About Ballroom dancing

Ballroom dance is a set of partner dances, which are enjoyed both socially and competitively around the world, and growing all the more popular in North America.

In this city, there is a moderately sized but strong ballroom dancing community, especially near the central Austin area. Numerous A number of dance studios in Austin offer ballroom dance classes, and if you are a UT student, UT has large student organization dedicated to ballroom.

Popular Dances

Waltz

In waltz, couples dance in triple time around the dance floor counterclock wise. There are many types of waltzes to dance to around Austin such as Country, American Smooth, Viennese, and Cross-Step Waltz.

Tango

This beautiful dance is danced between the leader and follower in an open or closed embrace. Common styles of tango danced in Austin are the Argetine, American Smooth, and International. Tango music typically contains musical instrument such as the accordian or violin, but there are fusion music that combines traditional with electronica.

Foxtrot

Foxtrot is also a progressive dance like waltz but is danced to 4/4 time rather than the waltz's triple meter. Typically danced to big band music, the basic steps for this flowy dance include turns, the three step, and the feather step.
